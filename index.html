Features

  • World-wide payment

    Due to the ability of communicating among chains, Roburst Coin is capable of payment anywhere, anywhen.

  • Multi-signature

    Multi-signature helps organizations or individuals to make a wallet that require an amount of private key to perform the transaction.

  • Decentralized

    P2P connection keeps the network from being centralized, also with our consensus algorithm, there is no major server at all.

  • Cross-chain

    RoburstBridge serves as a medium which help communications between chains easier than ever.

  • Secured

    Roburst Network offers many security options, from mobile to desktop applications.

About

how it began

This project began in 2015, when we think of an alternative of Bitcoin with the ability of communicating between chains and improvement in speed as well as security. With the help of our advisors, we promoted our method at a meetup in California in October 2017 and received a lot of interests from the community. The Roburst Network is the final product that we, the Roburst Team could bring our value to the world.

our mission

Our mission is promoting new way of payment, still keeping the network being decentralized and secured, also bringing improvements to the network. Bring more value to people through Roburst Coin and all other services in Roburst Network.
